White Space and Clean Design

Jane.com

Jane.com

White space is very important it helps with bringing attention to the design. It helps make our eyes pay attention to what is most important for the design. Jane.com has some nice examples of white space and having a clean design. It is very welcoming and is easy to view as a customer.
