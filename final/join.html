Join Us Today!

The Following are our Membership Levels with different perks! It is a great way to be involved in the local business community. Becoming a member is easy, we will get back to you within 48 business hours once you push submit!

Membership Levels

Nonprofit - $100

  • Business name, website, and social media links on our website directory
  • Any events you host will be advertised on our upcoming events page
  • Half price on standard booths for our 4th of July celebration
  • Join us for our lunch event every year
  • Join us for our big annual event 20% off

Bronze - $200

  • Same perks as previous membership
  • Half price to place an ad in our monthly e-newsletter
  • Join us for our lunch event every 6 months
  • Join us for our big annual event 50% off
  • Ability to have half price float entries for our Christmas light parade every year

Silver - $400

  • Includes perks listed from the 2 memberships
  • Join us for our lunch event every 3 months
  • List job openings on our website and in our monthly e-newsletter
  • 3 free tickets to our big annual event
  • Half off swag at our big annual event

Gold - $1000

  • Including all perks from above
  • Join us for our monthly lunch event
  • 4 free tickets to our big annual event
  • Chance to feature your business in our e-newsletter once a year
