Join us Today!

Why should you join us? There are many perks to each our our Membership Types. The Nonprofit has perks of staying informed on directory listings, social media information. The Bronze has all of the perks of the Nonprofit Membership, however there is more. You can receive luncheon tickets every month in a drawing. The Silver adds more onto the two previous levels and adds on some sponsorships for advertising. The Gold has monthly luncheons where all businesses can come together and learn from each other and earning a prize of discounts for lunches for your business.
