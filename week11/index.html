Idaho Facts

Idaho grows the most delicious potatoes. The state bird is a Mountain Bluebird. In Preston Idaho they have a festival called Napoleon Dynamite. Soda Springs is the largest man-made geyser, it was made in 1937 while engineers were looking for a hot water source for a swimming pool. The drill unleased the geyser. Fish Haven used to be called Rush Creek. Many people, who visit, fall in love with the view of the town.
