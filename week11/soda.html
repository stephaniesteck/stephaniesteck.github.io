Soda Springs like Geysers

By Samuel Hansburger

When you think of Soda, you think about the sticky, bubbly drink that many people have. This Soda is not the sticky bubbly drink, it is a Geyer. A man made one to be exact. Pressure gets built up and it looks like it explodes. What a fun nature phenomenon. Kids love to watch it.

You don't want to get too close, you would get burned. You have to keep your distance from it. It is cool to see from farther away but don't get close to it. No matter what it will be a fun thing to come and experience if you have never experienced watching a geyser before.

Many people come to watch it and hear it. The sounds you will hear when you come will be water rushing, voices of others who come to see it, hearing some nature especially when it is not crowded. This is something you don't get to experience much, unless you live close by or come to experience it in other ways.
