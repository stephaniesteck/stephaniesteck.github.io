Sunshine is good for the soul

By Preston Bennett

Sunshine is so great for our mental health. The sun's warmth on the skin and the vitamin D helps us all feel better. Sunshine is good for the soul. Sunscreen helps us from getting sunburned and aging our skin and have more risk of getting skin cancer.

Summer sun and a day on the lake fishing is what relaxation is all about. Listening to the water hitting the shore and the wind; watching the fishing pole waiting for fish to bite. Sitting in a camping chair or on a boat while fishing. Seeing the trees and lake.

The sounds are very relaxing around the lake, even if you aren't fishing, just listening to the music that nature makes, the birds chirping, the wind blowing into the leaves, and the sound of little ripples the lake makes. Hearing the fish jumping in the lake.
