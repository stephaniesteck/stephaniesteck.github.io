Target Audience and Scenarios

No matter what the plans are for your day or for your trip it is good to know if you need to pack for winter weather, hot summer, or something in between.

Winter Wonderland

Becca is an adventure loving single woman, she is in her mid-20's. She does photography as a hobby and to look for the beauty in nature and in those she takes candid pictures of. She has been waiting to take some pictures in the snow of her friends, a day that is partly and somewhat warm to get some good lighting and so her hands don't freeze holding the camera. She and her friends have been wanting to take a trip to Canada during the winter time to enjoy the nature there and she was looking at what days look the best for those pictures she has been wanting to take. She came to weathersunshine.com to find out what those days would look like. When they got there she was so happy that we were able to help them get those pictures, she ended up emailing us the first day and couldn't believe how beautiful her pictures had turned out, and how accurate our prediction was for that day and couldn't wait to get to taking more the following 3 days.

Spring Warmth

Sam and Jason are newlyweds, early 20's who love the outdoors, but also love to read while enjoying nature. They were looking for a weekend getaway after their finals where they could enjoy each other's company in nature and read their books together while cuddling. They were looking into going close to home during the spring time. They were able to find a good weekend to go to be able to enjoy their time in nature and get some vitamin D from the sun.

Summer Heat

Joe and Sally, who are in their early 30's, and their young kids were looking to go to the beach during the summer time, they wanted a day where there would be a slight breeze and hardly any clouds in the sky, but something that their kids could enjoy the day without too much sand getting into their eyes. They were able to find a week that was beautiful where they could enjoy time at the beach in the water and on the sand with their kids. They said the temperature was great for what their days on the beach.

Fall Nip

John is a 40 year old, who enjoys time with his kids and their friends hiking. They were looking for a day where they could go hiking to enjoy the nip of a crisp fall morning, that would turn into a beautiful cool afternoon where they could enjoy a nice picnic and enjoy looking at the falling leaves and the changing of the color from green to yellow, red, and orange. They were able to find a day where they were able to go and to enjoy nature and a hike.
