Typography

Typefaces and Content

There are many reasons why we use different typefaces for headings and paragraph. Headings help bring attention to what the following paragraph will be about. But also it can help show if it is a professional website, or one that is meant to be fun or creative.

Sample Text

Will I need to wear a jacket to my friend's party tonight?

That will depend on where you go, and what day. You can find that information by searching for the weather of the day and time.
