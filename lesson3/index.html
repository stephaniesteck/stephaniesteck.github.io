Weather Sunshine's Site Purpose

The purpose of Weather Sunshine site is to provide a quick and easy website to check what the weather will be doing for outdoor activities. For family activities and other outdoor adventures. No matter what it is that you are doing. This website covers all of Utah, Alberta Canada and California. We want you to be ready for your adventures with your family and friends and what kind of clothes to pack so you don't pack too many or too little based on the weather!
